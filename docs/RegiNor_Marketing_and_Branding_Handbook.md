## 7. Logo Usage (Conceptual)

*(Logo artwork to be defined separately)*

### General rules
- Logo should always appear calm and unobtrusive  
- Prefer monochrome (dark on light, light on dark)  
- Avoid gradients, shadows and effects  

### With tagline
Use tagline primarily on:
- Website hero  
- Pitch decks  
- One-pagers  

Avoid tagline in:
- Dense admin UI  
- Check-in screens
