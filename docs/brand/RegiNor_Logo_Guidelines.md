# RegiNor Logo Guidelines

## Logo Philosophy
The RegiNor logo should communicate **control, calm and professionalism**.

## Logo Variants

### Primary (Default)
- Wordmark: **RegiNor.event**
- Color: Primary text color (`#0F172A`) on light background
- Use when background is light and neutral

### Inverse
- Wordmark in white
- Use on dark or photographic backgrounds

### Monochrome
- Solid black or solid white only
- Use for print, stamps, QR stickers, invoices

---

## Clear Space & Spacing

### Clear space rule
- Minimum clear space = height of the **R** in *RegiNor*
- No text, icons or UI elements may enter this space

### Minimum sizes
- Digital: 120px width
- Print: 30mm width

---

## Placement Rules

### Recommended
- Top-left or top-center
- Website hero
- Footer attribution
- Slides and pitch material

### Avoid
- Inside dense admin tables
- On check-in confirmation screens
- As decorative background element

---

## With Tagline

**Allowed**
RegiNor.event  
*from signup to showtime*

Use only on:
- Website hero
- One-page pitch
- Marketing material

Do not use tagline inside product UI.

---

## Attribution Line

When space allows:
